I want to spend my life solving complex problems in healthcare by building proactive, scalable, and sustainable solutions that address the unique challenges of marginalized populations.

Accordingly, over the last few years, I've sought to train a 360-degree perspective through each project I pursue, understanding that one-size-fits-all medical solutions will not catalyze health improvements on a global scale.

I'm determined to optimize human well-being, constantly seeking ways to improve and uplift.

My dedication stems from my personal journey with type 1 diabetes, after being diagnosed at the age of 7. As I've grown up, I've transformed my relationship with the condition, recognizing that regulating nutrition, fitness, and sleep play vital roles in sparking my thriving state.

This approach allows me to lead a life where I'm confident that my potential knows no limits, and I aspire for my active and future contributions to allow patients to experience the same boundless potential.

Analytical thinking is at the core of how I approach problem-solving. I flourish when I can drill down to the atomic level of any problem, delve into the details, and uncover insights that enable me to connect the dots forward and backward.
